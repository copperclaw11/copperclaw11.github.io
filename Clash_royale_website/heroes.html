Season 79

What are Heroes?

Heroes are a new card rarity introduced in December 2025. They are enhanced versions of familiar troops, each equipped with unique abilities that can be activated during battle by spending elixir. Season 79 introduces Hero Wizard and Hero Ice Golem.

Unlock Requirements

  • 200 Hero Fragments - Required to unlock each Hero
  • Hero Fragments can be obtained from:
    • Hero Boxes
    • Special Events
    • Pass Royale (Arena 15+)
  • Hero Slots:
    • First Hero Slot unlocks at Arena 15 (5,000 trophies)
    • Second Hero Slot unlocks at Arena 25 (10,000 trophies)

How Heroes Work

  • Each Hero has a base elixir cost and a unique ability
  • Abilities cost additional elixir (typically 2 elixir) to activate
  • Heroes occupy separate Hero Slots (not part of your 8-card deck)
  • You can have up to 2 Heroes in your deck
  • Duplicate Heroes convert into additional Hero Fragments

üì¶ Hero Boxes & Fragments

Obtaining Hero Fragments

  • Hero Boxes: Special boxes that contain Hero Fragments and potentially Heroes
  • Events: Special events and challenges often reward Hero Fragments
  • Pass Royale: Players at Arena 15+ receive Hero Boxes in the Pass Royale
  • Duplicate Heroes: When you receive a Hero you already own, you get Hero Fragments instead

Progression Tips

  • Focus on completing events that offer Hero Fragments
  • Purchase Pass Royale if you're Arena 15+ for guaranteed Hero Boxes
  • Save Hero Fragments to unlock Heroes that fit your playstyle
  • Consider which Hero Slot unlock you're closest to (Arena 15 or 25)
