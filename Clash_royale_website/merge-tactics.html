Game Overview

Merge Tactics is a 4-player auto-battler mode unlocked at Arena 3. Each round has two phases: Deploy Phase and Battle Phase.

Deploy Phase

Spend elixir to summon or sell troops, merge identical units, and choose positioning. You can still buy and sell units on the bench during battle.

Battle Phase

Troops fight automatically. The losing player loses HP based on remaining enemy troops plus the ruler. Rounds repeat until only one player remains.

Ruler System

Advanced Merge Tactics Strategies

Early Game Economy

  • Start with cheap 1-star cards to build elixir advantage
  • Don't rush to merge - wait for optimal combinations
  • Buy cards strategically from the 20-card pool
  • Focus on building a solid foundation

Mid Game Merging

  • Focus on creating 2-star and 3-star units
  • Prioritize merging your strongest cards first
  • Manage your bench space efficiently
  • Adapt to your ruler's modifier abilities

Late Game Survival

  • Aim for 4-star units for maximum power
  • Prepare for the final 10-second damage boost
  • Deploy multiple high-star units simultaneously
  • Time your merges with opponent's elixir

Star Upgrade Tactics

  • Focus on one troop type for 4-star potential
  • Don't spread resources across too many units
  • Use 1-star units as fodder for higher stars
  • Plan your merging path from 1-star to 4-star

20-Card Meta Mastery

  • Learn all 20 cards and their optimal combinations
  • Focus on the strongest cards in the limited pool
  • Adapt strategies to the available card selection
  • Master the specific meta of Merge Tactics

Trait Synergy

  • Combine complementary traits for maximum effect
  • Use building traits for defense and control
  • Pair spell traits with troop traits
  • Leverage legendary traits for game-changing plays
