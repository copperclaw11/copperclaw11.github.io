Season 79

General Hero Tips:

  • Hero Selection: Choose Heroes that complement your deck's win condition
  • Ability Timing: Save elixir for key moments to activate Hero abilities
  • Defensive Heroes: Hero Knight and Hero Musketeer excel at defense
  • Offensive Heroes: Hero Giant and Hero Mini P.E.K.K.A work great in beatdown
  • Unlock Priority: Focus on unlocking your first Hero Slot at Arena 15

Hero-Specific Strategies:

  • Hero Mini P.E.K.K.A: Use Breakfast Boost early in matches to maximize leveling potential
  • Hero Musketeer: Deploy Trusty Turret to create defensive zones and split enemy attention
  • Hero Knight: Use Triumphant Taunt to redirect dangerous pushes away from your towers
  • Hero Giant: Heroic Hurl can disrupt defensive setups and catch opponents off-guard
